Double Glazed Windows

New UPVC double glazed windows are the perfect way to add value and improve the appearance of your Kent home. The Door and Window Co range offers so many choices that we guarantee to produce a window that will meet your specific requirements.

Double glazing choices include:

  • White or wood effect
  • Chamfered or decorative beading.
  • Leaded glass or plain
  • Georgian bar or bevelled.

We also supply triple glazed windows and the latest Residence 9 products. Feel free to check out our brochures page to see a more comprehensive listing of our products.
